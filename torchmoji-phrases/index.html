What is this?

This is a collection of phrases for which TorchMoji confidently predicts a single emoji. For example, the phrase i'll percent!! gives a probability of 98.44% for :100: (💯). There are also phrases for which every emoji is predicted with low confidence (see "None" 🚫). For example, the phrase its layered connelly. gives a probability of 2.33% or less for every emoji.

The goal of these phrases is to understand which words are strongly associated with each emoji. The phrases might also be interesting inputs for systems that use TorchMoji embeddings.

Methodology

These phrases were designed for compatibility with 15.ai's emotional contextualizers. So, any token with disallowed characters was excluded (e.g. hashtags were excluded because # is not allowed). Similarly, 15.ai has a character limit of 200, so phrases were limited to 30 characters at most.

The CUSTOM_UNKNOWN and CUSTOM_URL tokens were arbitrarily chosen to be zq and www.w. The other custom tokens were excluded due to character restrictions.

The phrases were found by hill climbing, brute force, and heuristics (e.g. repeating the same word many times). There is definitely room for improvement with better strategies.

The exact emojis and shortcodes used mostly follow TorchMoji's text_emojize.py example, but some of the shortcodes are wrong (e.g. :musical_note: is actually :headphone:). In those cases, the original DeepMoji codepoints are used instead. If the original codepoint is not a color emoji, then a similar looking color emoji is used instead (e.g. the white heart emoji 🤍 is used instead of the white heart suit ♡).
